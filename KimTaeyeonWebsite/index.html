안녕하세요 - Hello,

I am 김태연 - Kim Taeyeon, a Korean singer. Welcome to my official home page!

I had been a trainee at SM Entertainment's Starlight Academy during my middle school years before debuting as a member of the agency's girl group, Girls' Generation, in 2007. Since then, I have risen to prominence due to the group's success on the Asian music scene and further participated in the agency's projects Girls' Generation-TTS, SM the Ballad, and Girls' Generation-Oh!GG.

Aside from group activities, I have also recorded songs for various television dramas and movies.
